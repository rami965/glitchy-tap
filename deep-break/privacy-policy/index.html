Privacy Policy for Deep Break

Effective date: May 3, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Deep Break mobile game ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use it. The App is developed and published by GlitchyTap.

1. Summary

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Deep Break is a single-player offline arcade game. It works entirely on your device and does not require an account, an internet connection, or any sign-in.

2. Information We Collect

None. The App does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Account information or login credentials
  • Device identifiers (advertising ID, IMEI, MAC address)
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports sent to a remote server

The App does not contain any third-party SDKs that collect data, including (but not limited to) Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Facebook SDK, AppsFlyer, Adjust, Unity Analytics, or advertising networks.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App saves your game progress and high scores locally on your device using the operating system's standard local storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device.
  • Is not transmitted to us or to any third party.
  • Is automatically deleted if you uninstall the App or clear the App's data through your device settings.

4. Permissions

The App does not request any sensitive runtime permissions. Standard system permissions automatically granted to all apps (such as access to the App's own storage area) are used solely for saving local game progress as described above.

5. Advertisements

The App does not display advertisements and does not use any advertising SDKs.

6. In-App Purchases

The App does not offer in-app purchases.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). Because the App collects no data at all, no special handling is required for users of any age.

8. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect or process personal data.

The App is distributed through the Google Play Store, which is operated by Google LLC. Google may collect information about your download and installation of the App according to its own privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy. We do not control and are not responsible for Google's data practices.

9. Security

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data at risk of breach on our side. Data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's own security features (lock screen, encryption, etc.).

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
